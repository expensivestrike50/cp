Powering Africa’s Circular Future

Rooted in Ghana’s farming communities, Cocoa Potash tackles one of agriculture’s biggest challenges, reducing waste and regenerating the land,while delivering affordable organic fertilizer for farmers and high-purity, ESG-compliant organic potash for industry. We strengthen agricultural and industrial supply chains to drive Africa’s circular economy forward.

We Partner directly with farmers to convert millions of kilograms of agricultural waste into sustainable, high-value products that cuts emissions, and supply industries with responsible inputs for farming, manufacturing, and technology. Our vision is to pioneer Africa’s organic potash economy and set a global blueprint for transforming waste into progress, restored ecosystems, and empowered people.
