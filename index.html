Pioneering Africa's Circular Economy

At Cocoa Potash, we see opportunity where others see waste. We are a social enterprise born from the rich soils of Ghana, dedicated to solving one of agriculture's biggest challenges. By partnering directly with farming communities, we've developed an innovative process that converts millions of kilograms of raw agricultural waste—a source of pollution and crop disease—into high-value products.

This creates a virtuous cycle: farmers gain a new source of income from their waste, our planet benefits from reduced emissions, and industries gain access to sustainable, high-purity materials. It's a model that regenerates the land, empowers people, and redefines the value chain from the ground up.
